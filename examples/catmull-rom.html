Catmull-Rom

The green line represent a normal Catmull-rom spline. This implementation also allows tension values but not this is not the same as cardinal spline.

  • Green line demonstrates standard catmull-rom placing m0 and m1 at midpoints of p0 and p1
  • Yellow line demonstrates a m0 and m1 scale of 0 placing full tension
  • Blue line demonstrates a m0 and m1 scale of 1
